Hot dawgs for those that keep it real

Hot dogs are great because they are sort of tasty and easy to make. Spice it up from time to time and add new condiments like spicy ketchup, relish, cheese, and onions... now I really want a hot dog.

Condiment options

  • cheese
  • spicy ketchup
  • grilled onions
  • nacho cheese
  • More condiment ideas later! Stay tuned.

Instructions

  1. boil 2 cups of water in a shallow pot
  2. place hot dogs in water for maybe 5ish minutes
  3. take out hot dogs. Pro-tip: Don't use your hands for this
  4. place on a bun
  5. add condiments
  6. ENJOY
